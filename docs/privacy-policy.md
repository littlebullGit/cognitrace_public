# CogniTrace Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-05-02**

CogniTrace is a research prototype built for the Gemma 4 Good Hackathon. It runs entirely on your device.

## Data we collect

None. CogniTrace does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

## What stays on your phone

Everything. Voice recordings, extracted acoustic features, risk scores, saved screening results, and AI-generated narratives are stored locally in the app container on your device. They never leave the phone unless you choose to share a result or doctor discussion guide through the iOS share sheet.

## What we transmit over the network

Exactly one thing: on first launch, CogniTrace downloads a public, open-source AI model (approximately 2.7 GB) from HuggingFace. This is a standard HTTPS download of a public file. Recording and scoring can run before the download finishes. The AI explanation becomes available after the model is downloaded, and then works without an internet connection.

## Data you control

- Completed screening checks are saved locally so you can review history, rerun analysis, and delete checks later
- All saved checks can be deleted by you at any time via the in-app History view
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data
- There are no accounts, no passwords, and no cloud backup

## Microphone access

CogniTrace requests microphone access to record short voice samples for on-device analysis. Audio never leaves your device. Completed checks are saved locally in app storage and can be deleted from History.

## Third-party services

CogniTrace integrates no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking libraries.

## Children's privacy

CogniTrace is a screening tool designed for adults 50 and older. It is not intended for children under 13.

## Not a medical device

CogniTrace is a research prototype and is not a medical device. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or health condition. Results should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice.

## Open source

The full source code for CogniTrace is publicly available at https://github.com/littlebullGit/cognitrace_public under the MIT license.

## Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the CogniTrace GitHub repository at https://github.com/littlebullGit/cognitrace_public/issues.
